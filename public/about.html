What our clients normally ask

Design & Development Process

We design and develop every project from scratch. We first like to have a good understanding of what you're looking for, then we proceed to create the first draft for your approval. Once you have approved, we move on to the next stage which is where most work is done, considering any adjustments you might've requested.

Our processing time

Each project is different and while some may take us less time than others, we will always try to deliver your project within 10 business days. If for any reason there are delays, we will make sure to communicate it to you. In most cases we deliver within a week's time.

How do i pay and payment method

Because of the nature of our business and to provide services internationally, we use Payoneer. Payoneer is the world’s go-to partner for digital commerce, everywhere. They are secure and handle all private information safely. The accepted payment methods are Credit & Debit cards from Visa, Mastercard and American Express.

What is our refund policy

In any event, any payments made to BeDesigns for a project shall not be subject to refund after delivery of the initial design concepts are approved, or a change is requested, unless BeDesigns cancels or terminates your contract for any reason. For more details, please read our Terms & Conditions.
